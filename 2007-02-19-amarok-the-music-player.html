Amarok, the Music Player

2007-02-19 10:57:46 GMT

This is probably the first time I use Amarok with the Xine engine. I love Gstreamer's architecture, but it isn't there yet regarding stability and performance. I generally prefer using GNOME applications to do my tasks, but Amarok is so damn usable that it's really a surprise. It's a very mature and usable application and I use it daily since my update to Edgy.

Kudos for the Amarok developers!
