Entagged History

2005-10-18 17:05:40 GMT

As far as I know Entagged, written by Raphael Slinckx is the most advanced audio tag handling library that exists today. It started out as tag editor, but its library became very powerful. Initially it was written in Java, but its author later ported it to C# which became entagged-sharp and eventually it had made its way into Beagle. Unfortunately entagged-sharp currently doesn't support tag writing as entagged does, it only supports tag reading, but it's still very usable in this early stage anyway.
