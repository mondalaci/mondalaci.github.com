Catching up with the News

2007-01-22 02:31:52 GMT

I've successfully catched up with the news in the past couple days. I was lagging behind by about two months, the longest lag I ever had regarding the news that interest me. It was because I realized how inefficiently I was reading news. As none of the existing PHP feed readers satisfied my tasty needs, I created my own reader which I now use and it perfectly fits my needs.

I was thinking of releasing it, but doing so properly would require much larger efforts than making a plain tarball from it and I don't want to invest that much time into it yet. Maybe I'll do it in the future, because I think it's a really great piece of software even if it has some missing functionality and is a bit immature.
